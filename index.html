Netflix TV Shows and Movies Data Frame Analysis

Our dataset was taken from Kaggle.(https://drive.google.com/file/d/1CzWjuyO4F6nWwo5CJ98NakWaFIu92Jzd/view?usp=drive_link) We picked this dataset because all of us having different streaming service subscriptions so this topic choice is important to us personally. Additionally, with the advent of streaming services versus cable television, the use of streaming versus cable is definitely being debated globally. The proliferation of password sharing and streaming fatigue calls into question the long term sustainablity and profitablity of streaming services overall. With the rise of streaming services, platforms such as Netflix can use this data to refine its content offerings.
